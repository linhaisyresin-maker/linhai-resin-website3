SX-890X

Special Resin for Nitrate Removal

SX-890X is a styrene-based strongly basic anion exchange resin with a macroporous structure. Owing to its specific manufacturing process and active functional groups, it exhibits an excellent selective removal capacity for nitrate and nitrite ions in aqueous solution under the condition of coexisting sulfate (SO4²⁻), chloride (Cl⁻) and bicarbonate (HCO3⁻) ions.

Applications

SX-890X resin is widely used in industrial wastewater treatment for total nitrogen reduction. Featuring a macroporous structure, this resin has superior pollution resistance to low-molecular-weight organic molecules in water, as well as excellent impermeability and mechanical strength.
