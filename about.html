About Linhai Resin

Jiangsu Linhai Resin Science And Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in 1972, serving as a core Director Unit of the China Ion Exchange Resin Industry Association.

With over 50 years of continuous innovation, our facility covers an area of 75,000 square meters, boasting an annual production capacity of 25,000 cubic meters. We manufacture over 200 varieties of macroporous, gel-type, and specialty chelating resins.

Our products solve complex purification challenges in power generation, water treatment, biopharmaceuticals, metallurgy, and food processing across more than 100 countries globally.
