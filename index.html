COMPANY PROFILE

Jiangsu Linhai Resin Science And Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in 1972. As a director unit of the China Ion Exchange Resin Industry Association, we bridge the gap between complex polymer chemistry and industrial applications.

  • Capacity: 75,000 m² facility with an annual output of 25,000 m³
  • Compliance: EU REACH registered, drinking water & food hygiene certified
